    About Me

    I'm Ashlynn Landry, a 20-something-year-old building a career for myself in Front End Web Development. Born in Baton Rouge, raised as a Georgia peach, and graduated with a BBA from Ole Miss, my heart lies in many places, primarily with my dog Jamie, pottery, and design.

    Currently working as a Marketing and Communications Coordinator at Good Samaritan Health Center in Atlanta, creating branded graphics, materials and working closely with their website. There, I grew a liking for web design and development, which is what brings you and me here today!

    With my background in marketing and a love for graphic design, this portfolio showcases the skills I have learned thus far, and I hope that it will enable me to find a job to utilize my strengths and grow in the world of web development.
